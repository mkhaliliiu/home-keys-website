About This Property

Welcome to this exceptional signature villa nestled on the iconic Palm Jumeirah — one of Dubai's most prestigious and globally recognised addresses. This magnificent five-bedroom residence commands a prime position on Frond K, offering uninterrupted sea views, direct private beach access, and the ultimate in coastal luxury living.

Spanning 8,200 square feet across three floors, the villa has been meticulously designed by award-winning architects to blend contemporary aesthetics with Arabian coastal sensibility. The ground floor centres on an open-plan living and dining area that flows seamlessly onto the pool terrace and private beach through floor-to-ceiling bi-fold doors — creating a rare indoor-outdoor experience that few properties in Dubai can offer.

The chef's kitchen is a masterpiece of Italian craftsmanship, featuring Miele appliances, Calacatta marble countertops, and a separate catering kitchen for entertaining. Five en-suite bedrooms are distributed across the upper floors, each designed as a private retreat with walk-in wardrobes and designer bathrooms. The master suite commands the entire third floor with its own private terrace, a rain shower overlooking the sea, and a custom his-and-hers dressing room. The villa's outdoor space is equally impressive — featuring a 25-metre private infinity pool, poolside pavilion, fully equipped outdoor kitchen, beach sun deck and mature tropical landscaping maintained by the building team. A private gated driveway accommodates up to three vehicles alongside a dedicated golf cart for beach access.
